Kiriri Women’s University of Science and Technology (KWUST) is a private, women-only Kenyan university. Founded in 2002, its main objective is to bridge the gender gap in higher education while contributing to the country’s social and economic development12. KWUST offers market-oriented courses in science, technology, business, and computer science at its serene campus in Ruiru, Kiambu County. As a Christian-based institution, it empowers women through scientific education based on Christian principles
